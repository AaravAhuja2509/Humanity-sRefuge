Top 10 Exoplanet Candidates

These planets have been selected as the most promising candidates for our new home. Humanity must choose wisely before it's too late.

1. Kepler‑442 b

Location: ~1,196 ly, Lyra

Why: Earth-sized, rocky, in habitable zone (ESI ~0.84)

2. Kepler‑452 b

Location: ~1,400 ly, Cygnus

Why: Earth’s cousin; mild greenhouse potential

3. Kepler‑22 b

Location: ~620 ly, Cygnus

Why: First habitable zone planet found by Kepler

4. Proxima Centauri b

Location: 4.2 ly, Centauri system

Why: Closest possible habitable world

5. LHS 1140 b

Location: ~40 ly, Cetus

Why: Rocky, dense, cool super‑Earth

6. K2‑18 b

Location: ~124 ly, Leo

Why: First habitable zone planet with detected water vapor

7. Gliese 581 g

Location: ~20.4 ly, Libra

Why: Long-debated, possible Earth-like mass and temperature

8. Ross 128 b

Location: ~11 ly, Virgo

Why: Quieter red dwarf; good for long-term habitability

9. TRAPPIST‑1 e/f/g

Location: ~39 ly, Aquarius

Why: Multiple rocky worlds in habitable zone

10. Teegarden’s Star b

Location: ~12.5 ly, Aries

Why: One of the most Earth-like scores on habitability index
